Best Business For You

We divided the businesses in our data into eight main categories as per their industry. These categories are

  • Activities
  • Beauty
  • Food
  • Health
  • Nightlife
  • Pets
  • Services
  • Stores.

The wordclous describe the top 50 attributes that make a business successful in its category.

The wordclouds are followed by graphs. These graphs tell which are the top 10 useful businesses in a specific area for each category.

We give examples of two cities, Indianapolis and New Orleans.

Maps after the graphs can be accessed by pressing the "Show" button.

These maps include every business we have analysed to be successful and a quality service, and also have received a rating of at least 3.5 stars - the best businesses.

When hovering over a location tag, a business information popup is shown. The information is in the following order

  • Name
  • Stars
  • Attributes
  • Subcategories.
